Vehicle Tracking

Overview

Your Wave Device has the ability to track your vehicle. Wave is also pioneering vehicle tracking using USSD, a cellular channel which is subscription free.

How it Works

Your Home Hub™ gives you an stylish dashboard to track your vehicle. Which can also be accessed remotely via the free remote access app. The unit in the vehicle is fitted with a USSD Sim Card, supplied by Myriad Group. The unit in the vehicle will broadcast the exact location periodicly to your Wave Home Hub™ (via Myriad Groups Thingstream.io API).

The Result

While in your home anyone authorised to connect to your Wi-Fi can access the Wave Home Hub™ Dashboard and the Vehicle Tracking Feature, to view your vehicles location and battery level. You can also access the Home Hub™ dashboard (and subsequently the Vehicle Tracking Feature) from your mobile from anywhere in the world using the FREE remote access app.

Get Started

You will need two Wave units to track your vehicle. One connected to your internet router (Home Hub™ Mode) and the other installed into your vehicle (Tracking Mode). Wave can be ordered via our Products
