Freer Energy

With Wave fully operational in your home you will notice a dramatic reduction in your energy consumption. This is thanks to a number of great energy saving features. Firstly Wave itself is an all-in-one media server (with IPTV) it's also an energy monitor, vehicle tracker, wireless device controller and IP camera system. So instead of having a pile of devices in your home, each performing an individual task, Wave serves as an alternative to them all. And it is most likely more energy efficient than the existing single most energy efficient product in your home. Furthermore Wave gives you complete home control of wireless devices and energy monitoring of individual devices around your home (both locally and remotely). This makes it simple to see what's left on and simple to turn off. Since the service is locally hosted you can also schedule devices to turn on and off automatically, which further saves energy. And finally Wave is intelligent and so it can notify you of what and when to control devices or automate to improve your energy saving. Most recently we also introducted a technology to counteract your carbon footprint and avoid incurring carbon tax (enacts into US law in 2019).

Freer Internet

Once our network of Wave users reaches critical mass you will notice a new Opt-in feature which makes your internet free. Yes, Free Internet from Wave. This is a very incredible and groundbreaking feature of our Operating System. You will simply need to enter your account number with your ISP (if not detected automatically) and your bills from your internet company will stop. Look out for this new feature in the settings menu (accessible by selecting the logo on the Dashboard)

Faster Internet

In Home Hub Mode Wave connects directly to your existing router/ hotspot. But unlike your existing hotspot or internet connection, Wave is packed with a number of advanced technologies that speed up your internet. These include pre-configured local DNS Resolving and a Web-Proxy/ Cache. These jargon sounding features store queries you make for websites (and their public IP addresses) so that a second visit to a website from your home will load near enough instantly. Your entire online surfing experience will be transformed when accessing internet through Wave's Wi-Fi connection. Some users boast up to 5 times faster internet when connecting Wave to the standard router provided by your internet service provider.

Defence Grade Security

Wave is equipt with advanced security features. In addition to having a IP Camera surveillance system built in, the Operating System also features a family filter for the internet. Your internet is also cleaned via the built in firewall, preventing malware and malicious softwares from getting into your home and onto your devices. Wave was also awarded security patents from Google, since Wave is the first device to perform adreinsersion without breaching modern internet data protection and/or security standards
