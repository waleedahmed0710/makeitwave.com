Investments, Loans & Contributions

Wave® is the worlds first Free Internet & Energy Solution - researched, developed and financed by a community of British Forces Defence Communications Engineers. In 2016 Google's Patents Division awarded Wave® security & IoT patents and by 2018 Wave® is estimated to be worth between six and ten million US Dollars. Wave® is now launching a series of products which will be operated by WaveOS™ - Version 0.0.5+. This will help Wave® reach its first major milestone of 200,000+ users and an annual turnover of 100Million+ USD a year. Because of our past and future plans, Wave® is now able to offer serious rates of return for prospective investors and lenders. Our UK Company: Make it Wave Ltd (Trading as Wave®) is currently holding our Series A Funding Investment Round, to sell 15% equity of the company and raise 2Million USD in working capital. Wave® is also accepting contributions and offering lenders a great rate of interest of 10% APR per year. We also have a list of other ways businesses and people can invest, contribute and become involved in Wave® and its successes.


Wave® Micro Invest™
16.5% APR (5 Yrs)

Increments: £100 £1000
Info

Investing in Wave® helps our company and can earn our investors a 16.5% Per Year in interest. Your Wave® Lenders Account will be generated when you first make a deposit. To receive royalities from our network you will need our Wallet App. Your shares will be transfered to your wallet in the form of tokens and royalties proportionate to your shareholding will be sent to the same wallet. The royalties are in Etherium and can be transfered to a money exchange such as Kraken in order to release funds into your bank in whichever currency you prefer.

In the spirit of openess and transparency all transaction meta data (the date & amount) will appear in our public financial documents within 24 hours. Your Full Name, Payment and Contact Details will remain private and confidential. Any time you want to add funds to your Wave® Lenders Account, you can do so by visit this page and making a deposit. Your transactions will automatically be grouped with previous ones (under your corresponding surname/ alias) to produce statements.

Paypal is the merchant used by Wave®, so there also exists a trusted mediator offering dispute resolution and payment protection for all financeers. If you wish to provide an alias on your public statement, loan a lesser or larger sum, or pay by other means (cryptocurrency, bank transfer etc) then please contact us by email.
