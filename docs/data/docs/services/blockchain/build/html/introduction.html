Introduction¶

The Wave® Blockchain reflects all of the transactions between Token traders and our systems. At this time these transactions are infrequent and the system is still in development and so these records are being manually updates. This report will be published manually at various times throughout the year until we are comfortable to fully automate the reporting of this system.

Review & Purchase¶

The Wave® Decentralised Autonomous Organisation (DAO) is based on the Ethereum blockchain for public record. To purchase Wave® Tokens please visit the investment section of our website: https://makeitwave.com
