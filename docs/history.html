The History of Wave®

Wave® began in 2010 when UK Football Stadiums needed a team of highly skilled engineers to transfer their digital advertising solution from Bluetooth to Wi-Fi. This lead to a join-venture whereby advertising revenue from the network was divided with the football stadium owners. Wave® advanced this idea by applying it to a smart home device and sharing revenue it generated with the internet service provider, resulting in free internet for the end user. This innovation attracted considerable investment from the defence communications community and the project grew more serious.

By 2012 the challenges involved in making internet free were not just technical, but legal, philosophical and political. The business plan eventually evolved to incorporate the charter "internet access as a free and inalienable birth right". The closer Wave® has got to fulfilling this charter, the less perceived and more real the political threats became.

Then came the World Conference of International Telecommunications in Dubai, attended by the UN's International Telecommunications Union and 147 world leaders. This secret government meeting posing as an "open conference" underhandedly attempted to get world leaders to sign an unlawful and unconstitutional international treaty that would steer society into a distopia, the complete opposit direction of the charter set by Wave®. The UK and EU's elected "representatives" refused to present Wave® as a possible solution in its "open debate" - so Wave® got on a plane, attended the conference in person and confronted world leaders directly - placing the Wave® Whitepaper in the hands of every world leader, highlighting the illegitimacy of the international treaty and offering Wave® as the viable, lawful alternative remedy which was purposefully being witheld from the corrupted conference.

During a mid-conference TV interview with Terry Krammer (the US White House's Technology Advisor) it was said that the Wave® Whitepaper "caused a great deal of constination in the room" and "hijacked the debate". Wave® also made repeated requests for its Government back in Britain to interject and support the abolishment of this treaty - and in the last seconds it did: One by one (beginning with the UK, EU then US) Member States began questioning, rejecting and withdrawing from signing the controvertial treaty in favour of the charter presented in the Wave® Whitepaper.

Shortly after the WCIT in Dubai, many of the attendees partnered with corporations to launch their own free internet innitiatives and advances towards making internet a human right - but most have not survived the challeges and no longer exist today. To this day Wave® has maintained its course and made considerable advances, particularly in the area of security & privacy thanks to its background in cyber warfare. By 2016 Wave® had moved from London to a custom built campus in the Caribbean and increased its security from private military security contracts to a unifomed defence force. Wave® also secured patents and other contracts with Google and re-structured itself to became a one of three legs of a triad. The other two legs of the triad includes the Scottish Bay DAO and CCU. After incorporating free energy into its solution and increasing its defence, the charter was divided between each of three entities;

Wave®
Free Interet & Energy

Wave® operates strictly within the scope of its leg of this triad, focusing on the free internet and energy technology itself (software & products), supporting technical documents and service/ support.

The day to day work at Wave® is largely perfection and upkeep of the software, source code, supporting technical documents and its overal presentation. The goal is to reach the largest possible audience in the shortest period of time and maintain a stable and dependable network.

Scottish Bay DAO
Inherent & Inalienable
Free Interet & Energy

Internet and energy can now be made free using new technologies like Wave®, but this revolutionary technology is actually only part of what is needed for an actual revolution. Classroom Curriculums, jobs and laws are all still constructed around the custom and ritual of paying for our utilities each month. So there is equally as much legal and political work to be done as there is technical.

The Scottish Bay DAO is an Act of Union between administrators and professionals which are re-writing technical documents and rulebooks and drafting guidelines which will forge a society in which internet and energy is free, inherent and inalienable. Learn more

Caribbean Communications Unit
Official Defence Force
of the Scottish Bay

The third leg of the triad is defending the considerable investment and progress which has been achieved with Wave® and the work of the Scottish Bay DAO.

In 2016 the British Forces Communications Unit from RAF Akrotiri in Cyprus was revived and privatised to become Caribbean Communications Unit (CCU) - The Official Defence Force of the Scottish Bay DAO.

Agreements were reached between the British Landowners of CCU's Base of Operations, Google Loon and a local Solar Energy company (EVR Solar) to forge a safe surrounding for Wave® to continue its research, development of a free internet & energy solution.

Wave® has learnt many significant and valuable lessons on its journey so far. The entire concept of making internet and energy free is a technical undertaking not to be underestimated. The idea of introducing such a solution as a new right & freedom is also a dangerous game to play with dangerous forces and not to be lost. But after much time with all this it is safe to say with absolute certainty that the opportunities assosciated with pursuing this course of action are really of such incredible magnitude it's difficult to quantify.

Wave® is making internet and energy free and the software soltuion doing this is now open-source and free. Wave® also operates as a for-profit company, projecting $100Million+ turnover a year from 2020, which is only a fraction of the technologies handling of the funds passes between advertisers and internet service providers each year.

Google's first investor (Sir Michael Moritz) said in an email to Wave®: "Bravo, I admire, Pluck & Spirit". Google's Chairman and CEO (Eric Schmidt) followed Wave® on Social Media and liked posts. Then shortly afterwards Google's Patents division approached Wave® and awarded Wave® $2Million of patents, which they aquired from Motorola©. And Google's Secret division (Team X) flew to meet the Wave®/CCU triad before awarding our camp a Scottish Bay (& island-wide) contract for Google Loon. The Founder and CEO of Wave® was also head hunted to work at Apple in 2017. Wave® has set its sale price at $225Million, to be revised in 2020.

The software has been re-written as a script that can install to a device and configure itself fully-automatically (plug & play). The software is also able to self-update. With a few more improvements WaveOS™ will enable Wave® to exist in every home, quickly and globally.
