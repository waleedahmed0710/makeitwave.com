Role Store

Welcome to the Role Store, which is an abbreviation of the term 'Operational Role'. Unlike any other Operating System before WaveOS, Wave has a Role Store instead of an App Store. This is due to the fact the Wave Operating System actually gives you a choice of what function you want your device to perform before you arrive at the application layer. In the Home Hub Role for example, the Media Segment has its own Media App Store and the Home Control Segment has its own IoT App Store.

Home Hub Role

The Home Hub Role is presented as an option after your device has booted up for the first time with WaveOS installed. If selected WaveOS will autonomously install and configure your Single Board Computer to operate as a cutting-edge, Plug & Play, Smart Home Hub - featuring an All-in-One Dashboard containing a Media Center, IoT Smart Home Controls, IPCCTV DVR and interfaces for the Energy Monitor and Vehicle Tracking (if paired). Additionally there is subscription-free remote access from a free remote access app, downloadable on the Android Play and iOS App Store.
