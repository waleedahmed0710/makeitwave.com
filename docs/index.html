Introduction

Wave® is the worlds first free internet solution. The Wave Wi-Fi Hotspot has a built in 4G/LTE Modem which works on any network. The Wi-Fi Hotspot Menu (Dashboard) also features a multi-player game station and smart home media & control system. All of which can be accessed locally and/or remotely. The Wi-Fi Hotspot include most games consoles including the PSP, PS1 & 2 and Nintendo 64 & WII. And it features an IPTV Media Center, Smart Home Controls (IoT) and IP Camera Monitor. The controllers for the games are emulated, so they can be brought up in most web browsers on most mobile phones.

How it Works

Wave® reduces internet bills (by as much as 100%) by rewarding you for day to day use of the product. These rewards are in the form of cryptocurrency (Ethereum) coins, which are generated through ad-replacement and cryptocurrency mining as you surf the internet and enjoy the built-in applications. The funds generated automatically transfer from the built in cryptocurrency wallet, to your internet Service provider using a smart contract. The result is free internet access.

Getting Started

The 4G/LTE Wi-Fi Hotspot is built to be plug & play. Simply connect the Wave Hotspot to any power outlet and it will establish a connection with your local cell provider and begin broadcasting Wi-Fi speeds of up to 50Mbps of Free Internet. Pre-Order this Product Now.
